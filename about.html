Plant Care Web Application


My innovative web application is designed to monitor and manage the optimal growth conditions for my plant.
It tracks key environmental parameters including light level, soil moisture, air humidity and air temperature.
The system intelligently controls a water pump, turning it on or off to ensure my plant receives the
right amount of water when needed. This user-friendly interface allows me to monitor real-time data and manage my
plant care with ease, providing a seamless solution for maintaining healthy and thriving plants.
